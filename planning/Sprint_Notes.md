# Sprint Notes

## Sprint 1
During Sprint 1, the team conducted a planning meeting where all members participated actively. The main objective of this sprint was to define the sprint goal and ensure that every member contributed ideas and suggestions so the project could progress smoothly. During the meeting, the system actors were identified, with students as the primary actor and the admin as the secondary actor. The team then drafted the initial Use Case Diagram and Class Diagram. After completing the drafts, the team reviewed them together to identify mistakes and suggest improvements. Next, UI sketches were created and reviewed, followed by the development of early prototype screens based on the UI mockups. Test objectives were also defined and basic test scenarios were prepared. At the end of Sprint 1, the team conducted a sprint review and retrospective to evaluate the progress and discuss improvements for the next sprint.

## Sprint 2
After completing Sprint 1, the team moved on to Sprint 2, which focused on improving the prototype by implementing feedback received from the first prototype. Similar to Sprint 1, the team began with a planning meeting to define the sprint goal. During this sprint, user feedback was incorporated into the prototype, core system features were implemented, and navigation within the prototype was improved. Usability issues identified earlier were fixed, and test cases were executed to evaluate the system. Additional user feedback was collected and documented. The Use Case Diagram and Class Diagram were also refined based on the improvements made to the system. Test results and user feedback were recorded in written form. Throughout the sprint, project progress was monitored and updated, including the burndown chart. Finally, Sprint 2 concluded with a sprint review and retrospective to assess the outcomes and reflect on the team’s work.
